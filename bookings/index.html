BOOKING & RATES

RESERVATIONS

To book your shoot, please read the rate information and cancellation policy below, then follow the link to our online booking tool at the bottom of the page.

RATES

Fees for work are non-refundable, barring technical issues. Hair and makeup with will be reserved for all bookings and is payable separately.

ADULT PHOTO SESSION

Three to four looks: $690 Each additional look: $125

KIDS PHOTO SESSION

$440 for two to three looks.

HAIR & MAKEUP

Women: $180 for up to four looks ($30 each additional look) Men: $75 for up to four looks. Kids: rates vary by age.

DEPOSIT & CANCELLATION POLICY

A deposit of $200 for adults or $125 for children is required to complete your reservation. The deposit is not refundable, but may be transferred to a new booking if you rebook no later than 72 hours (three days) before your shoot. You may only transfer the deposit once. If you cancel your booking a second time, the deposit will forfeited. Changes or cancellations must be made no later than 72 hours (three days) before your scheduled appointment or your deposit will be forfeited. Call our booking manager to make changes or cancellations at the number indicated in your booking confirmation email.

PARKING & DRIVING DIRECTIONS

The studio is located at 922 North Formosa Ave. in West Hollywood. Parking is available on the north side of the building in the spaces reserved for Dana Patrick Photo Studio.
